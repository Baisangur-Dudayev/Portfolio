Hosting project (group project)

Project hosting and Network & OS Security
My role
In our project, I've been actively involved in several key areas. Firstly, I played a role in the physical setup phase, helping ensure that our infrastructure was configured and deployed effectively. Additionally, I've been busy exploring methods to orchestrate containers efficiently, contributing insights and strategies to enhance our platform's capabilities.

Furthermore, my involvement extended to helping with the deployment of VMware vSphere. Throughout the project, I've maintained a proactive approach, consistently seeking opportunities to contribute and add value. My dedication to staying engaged and proactive reflects my commitment to the project's success, as I continually strive to make meaningful contributions and drive progress.
Summary
This project is taking place within the framework of the Project hosting and Network & OS Security courses at Thomas More. In our group project, we're taking on the exciting challenge of creating a robust hosting platform tailored for MySQL applications, all managed through an intuitive API interface. This endeavor requires us to dive deep into the complexities of IT infrastructure and technology integration.

Our project involves setting up and fine-tuning essential components like servers, storage systems, switches, fiber switches, and firewalls. Moreover, we're implementing VMware vSphere to establish a cluster environment, ensuring our hosting infrastructure can scale and remain resilient. Security is a top priority, leading us to incorporate pfSense firewall solutions and OpenVPN for fortified network protection.

But our project isn't just about the technical backend. We're also developing a user-friendly website with advanced hosting capabilities utilising container orchestration. This platform will be the gateway for users to configure and deploy their applications seamlessly.
What did i learn?


Throughout this project, I’ve gained a much deeper understanding of how complex IT infrastructure comes together in a real-world scenario. Setting up the physical components gave me hands-on experience with the hardware side of hosting, something I hadn’t worked with in such depth before. Working with container orchestration taught me how important planning and structure are when designing scalable solutions. I also learned a lot by diving into VMware vSphere, not just how to deploy it, but how it fits into a broader ecosystem.

Beyond the technical aspects, this project highlighted the importance of teamwork. Collaborating closely with my peers taught me how to communicate clearly, divide responsibilities effectively, and adapt to different working styles. Solving problems together helped build trust in the team and pushed me to think from different perspectives. It made me realize how essential collaboration is in delivering a successful and well-rounded result.
